Jooby Avenue

SMD LED Streetlight

The luminaires are designed for road and street lighting, illumination of tunnels, squares, bridges, city public areas, industrial sites, parking lots etc.

The Jooby Avenue Luminaires are part of Jooby Smart Lighting Solution.

The Jooby Smart Lighting Solution ecosystem contains a full set of devices needed to build a smart lighting system: Avenue luminaires with control devices, outdoor motion sensors, control devices for third party luminaires, and the product list keeps growing. Please check our products list for more information.
